# Retentix

**Deterministic GDPR Execution Engine for Engineering-Led Teams**

---

## What It Is

Retentix is a headless execution engine that enforces GDPR compliance directly on production databases.

It executes **data retention**, **right-to-be-forgotten (RTBF / erasure)**, and **field-level masking** in a deterministic and auditable way.

This is not a dashboard, scanner, or SaaS platform.
It is an execution engine designed to be trusted in production.

---

## The Problem

Most GDPR and compliance tools focus on visibility:

* dashboards
* data discovery
* reporting

However, the **actual legal actions** — deletions, anonymization, and retention enforcement — are still:

* manual
* error-prone
* inconsistently audited

This creates operational and legal risk for both **Engineering** and the **Data Protection Officer (DPO)**.

---

## The Solution

Retentix treats compliance as code.

Compliance rules are:

* explicitly defined as policies
* validated before execution
* executed as one-shot, deterministic jobs

Every run either:

* completes successfully, or
* fails fast *before* any side effects occur

---

## What Retentix Does

* Time-based data retention execution
* Deterministic RTBF (erasure) with explicit cascades
* Field-level anonymization (hashing, nulling)
* Dry-run by default
* Full, tamper-evident audit logs

---

## What Retentix Does **Not** Do

* No UI or dashboards
* No data discovery or scanning
* No background services or agents
* No SaaS dependency or phone-home behavior
